Building Peace, Protecting Human Rights

Vision Peace and Human Rights Foundation is committed to global justice, equity, and the dignity of all people.

Our Mission

To protect and promote fundamental human rights, foster sustainable peace through dialogue, and provide humanitarian support to vulnerable populations.

Our Vision

To build a global community where peace prevails, human dignity is respected, and every individual enjoys equal rights.

Our Strategic Pillars

Knowledge

Intellectual Empowerment

We leverage education as a tool against oppression. VPHRF conducts high-level research and professional training for students, lawyers, and activists to master modern human rights advocacy and international legal frameworks.

Advocacy

Global Peace Advocacy

Our vision is a world where diplomacy triumphs over conflict. We intervene as neutral mediators in community and international disputes, creating platforms for sustainable dialogue to prevent violence before it escalates.

Legal

Justice & Legal Defense

Our legal experts provide pro-bono services to victims of injustice. We stand firm in ensuring the rule of law is upheld, helping the underprivileged navigate the judicial system to achieve true equity and fairness.

Response

Humanitarian Response

Beyond advocacy, we provide emergency relief to communities affected by conflict or disaster. This includes food, medical supplies, and psychological support for IDPs, ensuring every human life is valued.

Evidence

Research & Documentation

Expertise is built on facts. We specialize in documenting human rights violations with technical precision, providing credible data and reports that influence policy changes at national and international levels.
